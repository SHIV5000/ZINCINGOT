FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is the difference between SHG and High Grade Zinc Ingots? +
SHG (Special High Grade) Zinc has 99.995% purity, while High Grade Zinc has 99.99% purity. SHG is used for premium applications requiring maximum purity, while High Grade is standard for most industrial applications.
What is PMI Zinc and when is it required? +
PMI (Positive Material Identification) Zinc is certified through spectrochemical analysis to verify exact composition. Required for quality-critical industries like aerospace, nuclear, defense, and high-specification manufacturing.
What are Zamak 3 and Zamak 5 compositions? +
Zamak 3: Zinc 95.8%, Aluminum 3.9%, Copper 0.1%, Magnesium 0.03% | Zamak 5: Zinc 95.1%, Aluminum 3.9%, Copper 0.75%, Magnesium 0.03%. Zamak 5 has better mechanical properties due to higher copper content.
