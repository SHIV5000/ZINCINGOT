OUR STORY & MISSION

Established in 2010, Varman Metals has grown to become one of India's most trusted suppliers of premium Zinc Ingots. Starting from Meerut, we have expanded our operations across PAN India, serving major industrial hubs from Jamnagar to Kolkata.
Our mission is to provide industries with the highest quality zinc products that meet international standards while maintaining competitive pricing and reliable delivery schedules. We believe in building long-term partnerships based on trust, quality, and mutual growth.
