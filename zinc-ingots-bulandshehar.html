Yes, we actively supply to artisans in Sikandrabad, Khair, Gulaothi, Anupshahr, and rural areas. We have special programs for small artisans including flexible quantities and credit facilities for established craftsmen.
What is your delivery time to Bulandshehar brass clusters? +
We guarantee next-day delivery to Bulandshehar city and major towns. For village clusters, delivery takes 1-2 days depending on location. We have dedicated logistics for brass industry hubs.

Absolutely. We work with brass manufacturers to provide zinc with specific purity levels for different brass types - Red Brass (15% Zn), Yellow Brass (35% Zn), Naval Brass (40% Zn). We can customize zinc alloys as per your formulation.
