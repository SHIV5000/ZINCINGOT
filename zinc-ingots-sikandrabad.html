For most galvanizing applications in Sikandrabad, Prime Western Zinc (99.99%) is ideal. For premium coating requirements or critical infrastructure projects, SHG Zinc (99.995%) is recommended. We can advise based on your specific galvanizing process and client requirements.
Do you buy back zinc ash/dross from Sikandrabad galvanizing units? +
Yes, we have an active zinc ash buyback program for Sikandrabad and Secunderabad galvanizing units. We collect dross and ash from your facility, analyze zinc content, and provide credit against new zinc purchases. This helps in waste management and reduces your raw material costs.
